Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Stage Plot?

A stage plot is a graphic illustration showing where each band member stands, where instruments are placed, and what audio gear (like microphones, DIs, monitors, and power drops) is required. Stagex helps you generate this in seconds.

Why is a Technical Rider important?

A tech rider lists the exact technical requirements of a band or performer (e.g. input list, microphone choices, monitor mixes) to ensure the venue crew has the correct gear prepped and patched before soundcheck.

Is Stagex free to use?

Yes! Stagex is completely free and integrated directly into the Studio suite, allowing you to build, customize, and save plots and tech riders locally.
