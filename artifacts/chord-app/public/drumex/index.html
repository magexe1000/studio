Frequently Asked Questions

What is Drumex?

Drumex is a professional drum sheet builder, rudiment metronome, and visual practice suite built directly inside Studio, dedicated to training rhythm and execution.

Can I import custom rhythm sheets?

You can create, edit, save, and load your custom Snare drum sheet patterns right inside the browser-based editor. All settings store securely on your device.

Is the metronome audio precise?

Yes. Drumex utilizes highly precise Web Audio API schedulers rather than standard JS setIntervals, ensuring drift-free, sample-accurate, professional timing.
