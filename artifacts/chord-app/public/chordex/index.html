Frequently Asked Questions

What is Chordex?

Chordex is a robust chord library and collaborative songbook tool built into the Studio ecosystem. It helps guitarists and musicians discover fingerings, transpose songs, and sync setlists in real-time.

Does Chordex support offline use?

Yes! Chordex stores all your songs and chord libraries in local storage so you can easily pull up chord sheets on stage or in deep rehearsal spaces without an active internet connection.

Can I sync my chords with other band members?

Absolutely. By signing in with a Google or email account, all your devices and sessions synchronize in real-time using secure Firebase cloud Firestore databases.
