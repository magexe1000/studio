Frequently Asked Questions

What is Groovex?

Groovex is a professional multitrack practice player designed inside the Studio ecosystem, offering advanced stem isolation, looping, and pitch/tempo shifting controls.

Can I upload my own multitracks?

Yes. You can import your custom audio files for multitracks or import standard stems directly. All file parsing occurs securely and privately inside the browser.

Does it maintain high fidelity during slowdown?

Absolutely. Groovex integrates highly advanced phase-vocoder time-stretchers (powered by SoundTouchJS) running inside high-performance AudioWorklets for premium audio fidelity.
