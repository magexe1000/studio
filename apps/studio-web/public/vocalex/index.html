Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Pitch Tracker work?

Vocalex utilizes advanced pitch-detection algorithms (auto-correlation and YIN algorithms) via the browser's Web Audio API. It captures mic inputs, extracts fundamental frequency (Hz), and maps it instantly to musical notes.

Are my audio recordings secure?

Yes. All your vocal takes are processed locally in your browser. Audio files stay entirely on your device unless you explicitly sign in and synchronize data securely to your private cloud backup.

Which microphone should I use?

Vocalex works perfectly with built-in phone or laptop microphones, but for professional results, a dedicated external USB microphone is highly recommended.
